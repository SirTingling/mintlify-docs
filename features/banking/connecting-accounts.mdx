## What we can and can't access

When you connect a bank account, we receive:

| Data | What we use it for |
|------|-------------------|
| Account balances | Cash position, dashboard metrics |
| Transaction history | Categorization, reporting, reconciliation |
| Account info | Name, type, last 4 digits |

We **don't** receive:
- Your login credentials (ever)
- Full account numbers
- Ability to move money

<Info>
All bank connections are read-only. We cannot initiate transfers, payments, or any changes to your accounts. Technically impossible.
</Info>

## Connecting multiple accounts

Connect as many accounts as you need:

- Multiple accounts at the same bank
- Accounts at different banks
- Credit cards
- Both business and personal (if you need to track both)

Each account appears separately. You can manage them independently, and we won't mix your transactions.

## How often transactions sync

| Sync type | When it happens |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Automatic | Every 6-12 hours |
| Manual | Click "Sync Now" anytime |
| Initial | First sync imports up to 24 months |

New transactions typically appear within a few hours of posting to your bank. Not instant, but close enough for most bookkeeping.
